    What is DeepSquare?

    A platform to seamlessly interconnect users with decentralized supercomputing resources.

    Governance

    The DeepSquare project is a community led project, meaning that the community actively participates in streaming the decision making process with a system modelled on a community-owned smart contract protocol.

    Our Mission

    DeepSquare aims to support innovation in Europe and beyond and to offer a true alternative to the hyperscalers by providing world-class decentralised, responsible, sustainable, and managed High-Performance Computing (HPC) as an Ecosystem.

    Vision

    Empowering the professional cloud with the DeepSquare decentralised interoperable heterogeneous network in a sustainable and environmentally beneficial way.
