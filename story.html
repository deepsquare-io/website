    Empowering

    Sustainable and Democratized High-Performance Computing

    with DeepSquare

    DeepSquare is dedicated to making high-performance computing accessible to all, focusing on three core values: democratization, sustainability, and collaboration. Our aim is to challenge the dominance of major cloud providers by offering a fair and transparent alternative. We believe in a community-owned approach, ensuring that computing power is distributed equitably through innovative web3 tokenization. DeepSquare fosters a collaborative future by supporting projects within its ecosystem, promoting inclusivity, and striving for sustainability in every step of its operations.

    Our Origins: From Mining to Meaning

    In 2020, we ran a powerful 7MW crypto mining farm in Sweden with around 40k GPUs.

    However, we realized that focusing solely on Ethereum mining contradicted our core principles. This led us to shift towards impactful endeavors with genuine meaning.

    The Shift to High-Performance Computing

    The question was evident: "How could we redirect our hardware for a more meaningful purpose?"

    This prompted us to delve into High-Performance Computing (HPC), a field brimming with potential and in harmony with our mission.

    Embracing the Rise of AI

    Anticipating the rapid ascent of AI but also the absence of decentralized infrastructure, our team identified our true mission.

    With a team of experts in AI, cloud infrastructure, and blockchain technology, we possessed the knowledge required for a new venture – the creation of DeepSquare and its decentralized High-Performance Computing (HPC).

    Our Vision: Challenging the Status Quo

    DeepSquare emerged from the aspiration to provide a fair and transparent alternative to the dominant cloud providers such as AWS and GCP.

    We advocate for a community-owned approach, prioritizing sustainability in every aspect of our operations.

    Innovation through Tokenization

    We're transforming computing power with Web3 tokenization. Our ecosystem is crafted to distribute computing power fairly, efficiently, and transparently. At the core of this vision is the DPS token, evolving in tandem with our infrastructure.

    Fostering a Collaborative Future

    DeepSquare is more than just a platform; it's a thriving community. We actively support and provide grants to diverse projects within our ecosystem, fostering a collaborative and innovative future in high-performance computing.
